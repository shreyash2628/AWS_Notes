-What is cloud computing?
-cloud computing is on demand accessibility of computing power , database , infrastructure,softwares,storages and other IT resources.

-Types of cloud?
-i> Private cloud
 - Cloud service used my single organization , not used by general public.

 ii> Public Cloud
 - Cloud services/resources accessible by anybody over the internet and it is own and managed by third party services.
   ex-Amazon AWS.

 iii> Hybrid Cloud  
 - Keeping some services private and also taking leverage of public cloud together is called Hybrd cloud.
